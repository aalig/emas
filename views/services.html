PRAYERS

One of the core services EMAS offers is Prayer (Salah). There are separate prayer halls for men & women, with separate wudhu (ablution) areas. EMAS is a full time Masjid (mosque) that conducts the five daily Salah (prayer services), seven days a week, all year long. The following congregational prayers are offered:

  • Five daily prayers
  • Jumu'ah prayer
  • Janazah (funeral) prayers
  • Taraweeh during Ramadan
  • Qiyam Al-Layl during the last ten nights of Ramadan
  • Eid Al-Fitr & Eid Al-Adha prayers

  Islamic Funerals

  Family Counseling

EMAS provides spirtually based Islamic counseling with Imams or Muslim counselors/spiritual advisors. This service is provided for those who are seeking confidential and safe spiritually based Islamic counseling utilizing the teaching of the Glorious Quran and the hadith of the Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him.
