Ethiopian Muslims Association Of Seattle

Ethiopian Muslims Association of Seattle (EMAS) is a non-profit and non-political organization. EMAS is organized primarily and exclusively for religious purposes: for the protection and promotion of the religion of Islam within our community in the state of Washington.

EMAS — Services

Five daily prayers

One of the core services EMAS offers is prayer (Salah). There are separate prayer halls for men and women, with separate wudhu (abolution) areas. EMAS is a full time masjid (mosque) that conducts the five daily salah (prayer services), seven days a week, all year long.

Learn more »

Islamic Funerals

In the case of death, EMAS has the facility to accommodate the burial preparations. We also welcome our brothers and sisters of other faiths to join us during the actual janazah prayer (funeral prayer) to offer condolences to the family of the deceased.

Learn more »

Financial Assistance

EMAS gladly offers financial assistance to those who are in need. We simply ask that you provide us with the required information demonstrating your need for financial assistance. For more information please feel free to give us a call or email us.

Learn more »

EMAS — Programs

Adults

EMAS offers a Quran tafseer after Maghrib By Sheikh Abdulaziz M.Amin every Friday, a Hadith class after Maghrib By Sheikh Abdulaziz M.Amin and a Quran Tajweed By Ustaz Sa'ad Bahrezien starting from 5 till Isha every Sunday.

Learn more »

Children

EMAS offers different programs and activities for the youth childern. We have a Quran Tajweed and memorization and an Arabic study and Islamic history from 10AM - 2PM every Saturday and every Sunday.
