EMAS — Our services

Ethiopian Muslims Association of Seattle (EMAS) offers five daily prayers, Islamic funerals, financial assistance (Zakat/Sadaqah), family counseling and marriage services to the entire seattle and surrounding muslim communites.

 Five daily prayers

One of the core services EMAS offers is prayer (Salah). There are separate prayer halls for men and women, with separate wudhu (abolution) areas. EMAS is a full time masjid (mosque) that conducts the five daily salah (prayer services), seven days a week, all year long.

 Islamic Funerals

In the case of death, EMAS has the facility to accommodate the burial preparations. We also welcome our brothers and sisters of other faiths to join us during the actual janazah prayer (funeral prayer) to offer condolences to the family of the deceased.
For more information call us.
   (206) 243-1251

 Financial Assistance

Alhamdulillah, EMAS gladly offers financial assistance to those who are in need. We simply ask that you provide us with the required information demonstrating your need for financial assistance.
